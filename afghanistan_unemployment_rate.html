  • Afghanistan unemployment rate for 2021 was 13.28% , a 1.57% increase from 2020.
  • Afghanistan unemployment rate for 2020 was 11.71% , a 0.49% increase from 2019.
  • Afghanistan unemployment rate for 2019 was 11.22% , a 0.07% increase from 2018.
  • Afghanistan unemployment rate for 2018 was 11.15% , a 0.03% decline from 2017.
